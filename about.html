About Our Mission

At RC’s Custom Rigs, we are committed to crafting high-quality, custom fishing gear that elevates every angler’s experience on the water. What began in 2024 as a small husband-and-wife operation designing handmade popping corks has grown into a trusted source for premium lures, popping corks, gar leaders, and redfish leaders. Every product we make reflects our passion for fishing, our dedication to craftsmanship, and our belief that the best gear is built with both skill and heart. To know that their rig wasn’t mass-produced, but crafted by two people who genuinely love what they do and respect the fishing traditions that bring us all together. Our customers aren’t just buying gear — they’re investing in tools built to withstand the toughest conditions and help bring in unforgettable catches. As a family-owned business, we hold a deep commitment to the future. Our dream is to one day pass this company down to our children, teaching them the value of hard work, creativity, and tradition. RC’s Custom Rigs is more than a business to us; it’s a legacy in the making, built to be carried on for generations to come.
